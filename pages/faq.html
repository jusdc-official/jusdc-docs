General

What is JUSDC?
A USD-pegged stablecoin deployed on multiple blockchains.

Who built JUSDC?
Two dedicated non-technical founders (Dubai).

Is JUSDC audited?
Yes—CertiK (98/100) and Quantstamp (99/100) audits.

Technical

Which chains are supported?
Ethereum, Polygon, Base, BSC, Arbitrum, Orbix.

Are contracts verified?
Yes, all on respective explorers.

Reserve backing?
Audited supply and reserves—see transparency page.
