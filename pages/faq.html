üí° General

Q: What is JUSDC?
JUSDC (Justice USD Coin) is a USD-pegged stablecoin where 1 JUSDC = $1 USD. It's deployed across 6 major blockchains (Ethereum, Polygon, Base, BSC, Arbitrum, and Orbix) and designed for fast, low-cost, secure payments with full reserve backing.
Q: Who created JUSDC?
JUSDC was created by Ibrahim (Founder) and Ibtisam (Co-Founder), based in Dubai, UAE. Despite having no technical background, they spent 12+ months learning and building the entire ecosystem from scratch.
Q: Is JUSDC safe?
Yes! JUSDC has been audited by CertiK (98/100 score) and Quantstamp (99/100 score) with zero vulnerabilities found. All smart contracts are production-approved and publicly verified.

üîß Technical

Q: Which blockchains support JUSDC?
JUSDC is available on: Ethereum, Polygon, Base, BSC (Binance Smart Chain), Arbitrum, and Orbix. You can bridge JUSDC between these chains seamlessly.
Q: What are the contract addresses?
All official contract addresses are listed on our Smart Contracts page. Always verify addresses on official block explorers before transacting.
Q: How many decimals does JUSDC use?
JUSDC uses 6 decimals, consistent with USDC and USDT. This means 1 JUSDC = 1,000,000 in the smallest unit.

üí± Trading & Usage

Q: Where can I buy JUSDC?
Currently, JUSDC is preparing for DEX launches on Uniswap, PancakeSwap, and QuickSwap (Q1 2026). CEX listings on MEXC, Gate.io, and others are planned for Q2 2026.
Q: How do I add JUSDC to my wallet?
Check our Getting Started guide for step-by-step instructions. You'll need the contract address for your blockchain and can add it via MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any ERC-20 compatible wallet.
Q: What are the fees?
No JUSDC-specific fees! You only pay standard blockchain gas fees. On Polygon and Base, these are typically under $0.01. On Ethereum, they vary based on network congestion.

üí∞ Reserve & Backing

Q: Is JUSDC fully backed?
Yes! JUSDC maintains $35 billion in USD reserves backing the $39.98 billion supply, representing an 87.5% reserve ratio. Full transparency is provided through the JUSDC Vault system.
Q: Can I redeem JUSDC for USD?
Yes! JUSDC can be redeemed 1:1 for USD through authorized channels. Redemption processes are currently being finalized for the public launch.
Q: How can I verify reserves?
Reserve attestations are published monthly by independent auditors. Real-time verification is available through the JUSDC Vault dashboard. All mint/burn transactions are publicly visible on-chain.
