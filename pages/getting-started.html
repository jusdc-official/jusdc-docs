What is JUSDC?

JUSDC (Justice USD Coin) is a USD-pegged stablecoin where 1 JUSDC = $1 USD. It's deployed across 6 major blockchains and designed for fast, low-cost, and secure cross-chain payments with full reserve backing.

Supported Blockchains

JUSDC is natively deployed on 6 major blockchains:

  • Ethereum - Main network
  • Polygon - Low fee transactions
  • Base - Coinbase Layer 2
  • BSC - Binance Smart Chain
  • Arbitrum - Ethereum L2
  • Orbix - Ecosystem chain

Adding JUSDC to MetaMask

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open MetaMask and select your network (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
  2. Click "Import Tokens" at the bottom
  3. Paste the JUSDC contract address for that network
  4. Token symbol (JUSDC) and decimals (6) should auto-fill
  5. Click "Add Custom Token"
  6. Confirm and you're done! ‚úì
