What is JUSDC?

JUSDC (Justice USD Coin) is a USD-pegged stablecoin where 1 JUSDC = $1 USD. It's deployed across 6 major blockchains and designed for fast, low-cost, secure payments.

Adding JUSDC to MetaMask (Step-by-Step)

  1. Open MetaMask and select the network (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
  2. Click "Import Tokens" at the bottom
  3. Paste the JUSDC contract address for that network
  4. Token symbol (JUSDC) and decimals (6) should auto-fill
  5. Click "Add Custom Token"
  6. Confirm and you're done!
