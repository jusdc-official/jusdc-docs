mint()

Creates new JUSDC tokens when USD is deposited. Only callable by authorized minters. Each token is backed 1:1 by USD reserves.

burn()

Removes JUSDC from circulation during redemption. Triggers USD release from reserves back to user.

transfer()

Standard ERC-20 transfer function. Moves JUSDC between wallets with minimal gas fees.

pause()

Emergency security function. Temporarily halts all transfers in case of detected vulnerabilities.
