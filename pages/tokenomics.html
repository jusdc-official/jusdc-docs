üéØ Peg Mechanism

JUSDC maintains its $1 USD peg through a combination of reserve backing, arbitrage opportunities, and transparent minting/redemption processes.

Minting Process:

  1. User deposits USD to authorized account
  2. Deposit verified (1-2 business days)
  3. Equivalent JUSDC minted to user's wallet
  4. Reserves increase proportionally

Redemption Process:

  1. User requests redemption via CashMatrix or jusdc.io
  2. JUSDC burned from circulation
  3. USD transferred to user's account (2-3 business days)
  4. Reserves decrease proportionally
üí° Arbitrage Opportunity: If JUSDC trades below $1, arbitrageurs can buy and redeem for profit. If above $1, they can mint and sell. This keeps the price stable.
